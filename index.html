Why Fox24Coin?

Gold Reference

Fox24Coin takes inspiration from gold as a reference point, connecting digital assets with a traditional store of value.

Transparent Smart Contracts

All Fox24Coin contracts are verifiable on Ethereum, ensuring transparency and accessibility for the community.

Community-Oriented

The project emphasizes open communication and collaboration with its community through official channels.

FOX24 Tokens Overview

FOX24 Tokens are designed to combine modern cryptocurrency technology with the concept of gold-backed stability. While not directly redeemable for physical gold, they provide secure and innovative ways to participate in the FOX24 ecosystem.

FOX24 Base

The main token used for transactions and participation in the FOX24 ecosystem, ensuring liquidity and stability.

FOX24 ETH

ERC-20 compatible token for Ethereum wallets and DeFi integrations, expanding FOX24 utility in crypto markets.

Our Networks

Official FOX24 token smart contracts. All contracts are built on OpenZeppelin libraries, monitored live by Cyberscan and protected by Cloudflare.

Official Smart Contracts

As part of our multi-chain strategy, FOX24 Coin is deployed on multiple networks. Always verify contract addresses and their status before interacting.
