Project Roadmap

Our vision for the future is built on a foundation of tangible value. Each phase of our project is represented by a unique NFT, backed by a different precious metal, symbolizing our growing stability and commitment.

Phase 1: Gold

The Golden Standard

The initial phase establishes Fox24Coin's core value by backing it with physical gold. This creates a stable, reliable digital asset, merging the security of traditional wealth with the innovation of cryptocurrency.

Phase 2: Silver

Expanding the Treasury

In our second phase, we will introduce silver backing to diversify our precious metal reserves. This move is designed to enhance liquidity and offer new opportunities for investors, strengthening the entire ecosystem.

Entering the Premium Market

The third phase marks our entry into the premium market by incorporating platinum into our backing assets. This rare and valuable metal signifies our commitment to long-term growth and high-value asset integration.

The Pinnacle of Value
