Our Story: From Challenge to Innovation

Initially launched as Raccoon24 (RC24), we faced a devastating hacking attack that compromised our project’s security and branding. However, we used this challenge as an opportunity to come back stronger with Fox24Coin—a smarter, more secure cryptocurrency designed to outsmart threats in the crypto world. We are committed to providing a stable, trusted future for investors, backed by the security of physical gold worth 200,000 euros secured from a dedicated investor.
