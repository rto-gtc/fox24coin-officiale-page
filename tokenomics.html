Key Metrics

Our economic model is carefully designed to ensure transparent and responsible management of the entire token ecosystem.

Transparent Asset Management

Our allocation strategy is built on transparency and security to protect investors and ensure long-term sustainability.

  • Controlled Minting: New tokens can only be minted by the project owner, preventing unauthorized supply increases.
  • No Hidden Wallets: The project team holds no tokens in private wallets, ensuring there are no hidden reserves that could be used for market manipulation.
